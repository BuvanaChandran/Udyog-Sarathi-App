Udyog Saarthi App

The "Udyog Saarthi App" is a transformative online platform dedicated to empowering Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) by enhancing their access to vocational training, job opportunities, and career development. This inclusive platform not only assists PwDs in creating personalized learning profiles but also provides crucial information about government and private sector job vacancies, exam preparation support, and awareness campaigns. By bridging the information gap and offering tailored resources, the app aims to significantly improve PwDs' employment prospects, ultimately fostering economic independence and a dignified life for this deserving community.

Learning profile

Create your profile!!!

Udyog Saarthi's 'Learning Profile' empowers individuals with disabilities by offering a personalized roadmap for skill development and career growth. By creating a tailored profile, users can access resources, training programs, educational opportunities, and assistive technologies that align with their unique needs and aspirations. Whether it's enhancing skills, pursuing education, or finding the right support, the Learning Profile feature is the gateway to a world of customized opportunities aimed at fostering personal and professional development
